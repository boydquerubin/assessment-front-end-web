Rexburg, Idaho

Nestled in the heart of eastern Idaho, Rexburg exudes small-town charm while offering an array of attractions and opportunities for residents and visitors alike. Home to Brigham Young University-Idaho, the city boasts a vibrant college-town vibe. From exploring the scenic beauty of Yellowstone Bear World and the St. Anthony Sand Dunes to indulging in local cuisine at charming eateries, Rexburg offers a delightful blend of rural tranquility and modern amenities, making it a captivating destination in the Gem State.

Notable places to visit:

  • Sand Dunes
  • Golf
  • Rock Climbing
  • Gravity Factory
  • Parks
    • Porter Park
    • Smith Park
    • Nature Park
